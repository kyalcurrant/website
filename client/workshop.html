What You'll Learn

Practical tools for authentic presence.

This workshop is about more than speaking. It's about embodying your message so when you step onto a stage, your presence speaks before your words do.

Learn how to create a simple one-page speaker document for engagements that clearly communicates your value and story.

02

Develop a clear message based on your lived experience that resonates with your audience and positions you as an authority.

03

Develop a confident elevator pitch and social pitch to communicate what you do and why it matters.

Position Yourself

06

Speak with Clarity

Master the art of speaking with clarity, authenticity, and emotional connection that moves your audience.
